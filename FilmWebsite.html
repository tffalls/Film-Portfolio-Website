About

Taylor Falls is a Film & Media Production student at Arizona State University pursuing a BA with a technical focus. She is also working as a promotional video editor at AZ PBS. She has a passion for writing, directing and editing. She has worked on various student films, most recently she edited the short documentary 'Lead by Example.'

She is starting pre-production for her senior capstone project on a short film she wrote, and will direct and edit. The short film currently titled 'Dance With Me' is the story of Cathy Smith, a young teenager, is overcome with anticipation and excitement for her first high school dance; however, her dream becomes a nightmare as her father Fred decides to follow her into the gym and insists on her first dance. Cathy is mortified as she feels all eyes on her in conjunction with the mounting pressure to dance with her father. She breaks Fredâ€™s heart by rejecting him and storming off. Unfortunately, all choices have consequences and Cathy learns a painful lesson of regret. A decade later Fred has passed, and Cathy thinks back to that moment in the dance. She envisions everything exactly the same, but this time she says yes to dance with her dad and make him happy regardless of what others think. Yet, that is not how it happened and she is sorrowfully brought back to reality and left with the consequences of her actions.
